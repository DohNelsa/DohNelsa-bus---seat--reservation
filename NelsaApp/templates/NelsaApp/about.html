About MOGHAMO EXPRESS

Your trusted partner for safe and comfortable travel across Cameroon.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, MOGHAMO EXPRESS has grown from a small local bus service to one of Cameroon's leading transportation companies. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide safe, reliable, and comfortable travel options for people across the country.

Over the years, we've expanded our fleet, improved our services, and built a reputation for punctuality and customer satisfaction. Today, we operate routes connecting major cities across Cameroon such as Bamenda, Yaounde, Douala, serving thousands of passengers daily.

Our commitment to excellence and customer service has made us the preferred choice for travelers seeking a hassle-free journey experience.
