Our story

From local service to nationwide connections

Founded in 2010, MOGHAMO EXPRESS grew from a small local bus service into one of Cameroon’s trusted transportation names. We started with a simple mission: safe, reliable, and comfortable travel for everyone on the road.

Today we connect major cities including Bamenda, Yaoundé, and Douala — with a focus on punctuality, well-maintained vehicles, and service that respects your time.
